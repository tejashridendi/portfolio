### Projects
**Prediction of Student Performance**
[Visit Github] (https://github.com/tejashridendi/Prediction-of-StudentPerformance)
Trained on a Linear Regression model and  step-wise Linear Regression model using **R** to forecast student final grade in math “G3”. Trained and evaluated without tuning the grid  and found that Step-wise LR Model selected 2 best predictors "G1", "G2" concluding that G1, G2 are significant contributors in the prediction of G3. Linear Regression & step-wise Linear Regression model achieved an RMSE of 2.0 and 1.70 on test data respectively concluding  UCI’s student performance. 

**Dynamics of COVID-19's spread across the globe**
[Publication] (https://nebigdatahub.org/tejashri-dendi-covid-information-commons-student-working-group-data-visualization-project/)
Uncovered insightful patterns & conclusions about the pandemic's evolution and the effectiveness of different governmental strategies in curbing its impact using **Python**. The time-series analysis demonstrates distinct trajectories of new COVID-19 cases in China, the US, and Sweden. This variation is likely reflective of the differing national policies and cultural backgrounds of these countries affecting the spread of the virus. The geospatial analysis supports the notion that regional policy and culture have a profound impact on the spread of the virus, with these three countries serving as microcosms for their respective regions' broader responses to the pandemic.
